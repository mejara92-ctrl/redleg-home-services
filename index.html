FAQ

Quick answers before you book.

Do you only do garages?

No. Garages are our specialty because they create dramatic before/after results, but we also handle painting, lighting, storage, flooring options, and general home punch-list work.

Do you do electrical or HVAC?

We can help coordinate basic lighting upgrades and home projects, but licensed electrical, HVAC, plumbing, or code-required work must be handled by properly licensed professionals.

How do I get the fastest quote?

Text clear photos of the space, your address, the project goal, and your preferred timeline. Photos are the fastest way to avoid vague pricing.

Are materials included?

It depends on the project. Some quotes can include materials; others may separate labor and materials so you can choose your finish level.

What areas do you serve?

Lawton, Fort Sill, Elgin, Cache, Fletcher, and nearby areas. Travel fees may apply outside the normal service area.
